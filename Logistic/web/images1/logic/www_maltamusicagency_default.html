Dj Simona Gee

24.01.09 :: KU Club - Gozo
Simona Gee DJ  is in the musical italian scene since first nineties winning a dj contest and taking part, with an excellent place, at the important dj competition "D.M.C.".
She soon distinguishes herself at the consolle for her personal dj-sets and love for new sound brings her to continuous search of musical contaminations always in step with times and fashions.

She plays house with house  and hard house.

She has been guest in many clubs transmitting positive vibrations and very good musical selections.

On her artistic way she feels the wish "to create" music besides propose it and from consolle to recording room the step is short and she begins to collaborate for various productions.

She has selected and mixed songs for lounge and house compilations, the last for Match Music with 15.000 compact discs copies.

Her road crosses quickly the Italian frontiers and passing through Switzerland arrives in UK, where she begins to appear as special guest and to collaborate with important production teams, up to meet F.C.Hackett, charismatic american singer with a powerful and scratching voice, with which she decides to produce his new efficacious single "I Have A Dream" and she prepares to inflame the dancefloors all over the world.
The house-track has been composed and created with M.Gazzotti and A.Bagnoli at Alby Studio.
The This song will be included besides in the compilation track list "DJ Woman" enclosed to an illustrated book about women at the consolle, in collaboration with the Associazione Italiana Dj Donne, Fashion&Sound Management, the record company Melodica and the a.r. journalist and p.r. Riccardo Sada.

Dj Simona Gee will perform her set side by side with Malta's finest Alvin Gee, Aaron and Simon F next Saturday 24th January, 2009 at KU Club, Gozo. See you there!

Dj Ross

28.02.09 :: Sky Club - Malta
Rossano Prini a.k.a. "DJ ROSS" is born on August 13th 1973. His career begins at 17 years old, when he play as resident dj in different locals of north-Italy and in collaboration with some recording studios, as long as in the '93 he enters in the record world, departing from “Discomagic”, then to “Self Distribuzione” and then with Luca Mensi to “Email Records” and from the '98 he becomes A and R manager of “Time Records”, with which he creates the sub-Label “SPY Records”.

With SPY Records he discovers in these years different talents amongst the most important artists of the Italian and foreigner dance panorama. In these years at Time Records, Ross creates new important productions as THE LAWYER and EMAGIC feat. Nancy with Nick Ferrando and Stefano Marcato. It was born a collaboration with Tristano De Bonis and Luca Mensi; they creates MAGIC BOX and ERIKA, overwhelming the Italian and foreigners charts with their different tracks. In 2001 it begins the collaboration with Paolo Sandrini and it was born "Dreamland", the first success marked DJ ROSS, then follows “Emotion” and it begins the tours in Italy and in different countries of the world, creating the SPY PARTY with the other DJ ROSS’s artists, like S.M.S., M@D, etc. It follows the traks "Smile" and “Floating in Love", bringing him to be always present in the Italian and foreigners charts. In 2004 DJ Ross, Magic Box and Erika flyght to St. Paul in Brazil for the first concert live together, the "PLANET POP FESTIVAL" getting a big success; they bliss it in the year 2005 with a very big approval of the fans! In the meantime it was born the “LABEL73”, another sub-Label of Time Records taken care of by Rossano, which handles also with other kind of music, and success like: SQUEEZE UP feat. Rod Fame and TEISHAN "You Isla Bonita" and BEATS and STYLES "Dance Dance Dance" arrive! In 2005 DJ ROSS VS. DY "Get Up" is released (always in production with Paolo Sandrini and sung by William Naraine of DOUBLE YOU) and it immediately arouses a big interest with the Label partners, getting licenses in different countries of the world. Soon after the release of "Beat Goes On" comes. Both the tracks were released in the year 2006 all over the world.

Join us for this one-time event at Sky Club, Malta next Saturday, 28th February, 2009!

DJ SUGARSHAKER BIOGRAPHY

SugarShaker - DJ, Producer and official ‘Booty-shaker’ has only been Djing professionally for approximately 4 years. A relatively short time when you consider what she has achieved! She will be the first person to stand up and say ‘she knew nothing whatsoever about the music industry and Djing’ before she realised it was without a doubt her destiny! Being one of the few, new female DJ’s on the scene to learn to mix with vinyl, she is more than comfortable both with 1210’s and with the new CD mixers and is renowned for her love affair with the EQ’s!! Since her very first mix she hasn’t looked back and is extremely pedantic about her mixing and her sets and believes in
‘preparation, preparation, preparation to ensure that she delivers the best music she possibly can to ensure the dance floor is jammed!

SugarShaker picked up 2 residencies within her first 6 months of playing out; firstly a regular gig for the Internationally acclaimed Koolwaters, playing at The Beduin Bar in London and also at The Fez Club in Bristol, alongside head honcho Marc Vedo and secondly, for local night Housework,
dubbed ‘The best night in the South West’ by Mixmag! These residencies gave her the opportunity to play alongside some of the best DJ’s in the industry including Pete Tong, Dave Pearce, Fergie and Lottie (Radio 1) plus Tall Paul, The Trophy Twins, Oliver Lang, Kate Lawler, Rebekah and Big Al and Nathan Thompson (Kiss101FM) amongst many others! SugarShaker set out to rock
dance floors in as many towns and cities as possible and has been lucky enough to have had the pleasure of playing at some of the best clubs in the UK, as well as dipping her toe into International waters, playing in France, Marrakesh, Sharm el Sheikh, Ibiza and Corfu!

Not satisfied with rocking the dance floor with other people’s productions, SugarShaker very quickly realised that she needed to produce her own material in order to be taken seriously in the music industry. So she hooked up with the talented Leeroy K - who is also one half of LnM Projekt - and
got straight down to the nitty gritty, with their first track ‘On my way’, which was also written and performed by SugarShaker, being signed to the mighty Hoxton Whores’ label Whorehouse - it hit number 20 in the Beatport charts in it’s first week of release and stayed in the charts for a good few
weeks - plus the vinyl was also sold in all good record stores all over the world - done in true SugarShaker style!

Since then the duo have had a further track signed to American label ‘Metronix’ and have another 5 tunes in the pipeline so things are about to hot up over the next 12 months when hopefully all the tracks will have been released and SugarShaker will have truly made her mark on the industry!

Apart from the deep passion for House music that SugarShaker has, she also truly believes that the art of being a good DJ comes down, not only to tune selection and technical ability but also to the connection that the DJ has with the people on the dance floor - if you don’t get that connection, there may as well be a CD playing through a stereo - it’s the people that make the music - and the night and that is something which is always at the forefront of SugarShakers’ mind, so if you get the chance to see this lady spin a few tunes in a club near you, expect it to rock, from beginning to end!
