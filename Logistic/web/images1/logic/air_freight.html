    Air Freight

    Air freight is the quickest method of sending goods overseas and usage of  air freight is ideal for those items which your consignee requires immediately at destination. Air Freight Shipments are time sensitive and expensive and the only option for quick deliveries.

    Airlines charge on weight basis calculated from either the actual weight  or on volumetric weight of the shipment, whichever is higher.  (Volumetric Weight : 1 cubic meter = 166.66 kgs).
