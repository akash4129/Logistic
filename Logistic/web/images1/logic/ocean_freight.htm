    Ocean Freight

    Ocean Freight is a suitable solution for regular and larger freight without an emphasis on transit time but on saving shipping costs.  A major portion of the globally traded goods is shipped using Ocean vessels. This service is the best solution for delivering economically sizable loads of cargoes.  Thanks to the uniform system of internationally used shipping units "containers",  this shipping method is the most effective solution in freight forwarding.   

    The "container" is the universal shipping unit used today for ocean freight. According to the amount of shipped goods, frequently used container sizes are 20-feet, 40-feet,  and 40-feet High Cube.  Besides commonly used these dry container, there are also tank container, refrigerated container , frame container, platform container, ventilated container, livestock container, loose container, mounted container, and so on.  For calculating convenience, the twenty-foot equivalent is internationally recognized as the calculating unit, expressed as TEU.  When counting different specs of containers, the container length is converted into the unit of TEU.

    Our Services in Ocean Freight

     

    We offers the follwoing Services :-

    FCL – (Full Container Load) Service

    A single shipment that occupies the entire weight or cubic (space) capacity of one container to give  a cost effective merchandise to our customers. Another great advantage of this service is the security of goods against damage or theft as the containers which are sealed after loading by the shipper and transported with the same seal to the final destination

    LCL  – (Less Than Container Load) Service 

    When a Shipper does not have enough cargo for a full container, we offer less-than-container load (LCL) service so that the Shipper need not  to pay the freight for an entire container, but only for the space that  his cargo takes up in the container.
