About Us

 

Birkenstrasse 47, 6343 Rotkreuz


The LOEWENSTEIN - GROUP AG provides expertise and delivers tailor-made, unique financial, industrial and trading solutions specialised in heavy duty machinery in the field of oil, gas, petrochemical industry and (alternative) energy.

Build on our experience of more than two decades in metal, automotive, oil and gas industry, in the sector of energy and international trading our company contributes values to our customers. The LSG - GROUP AG is performing a systematically investigation, research and comparison of suppliers, products, capabilities and financial solutions to achieve the expectations of our clients.

Our selection of European manufactures bases on the LSG-Quality-Values (LQV):

  • Leading Technologies
  • Highest Quality
  • Highest Reliability
  • Best value for money
  • Best delivery conditions
  • Best finance-performance

 

We select and represent the leading suppliers and manufacturer of key machineries.
